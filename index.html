[Patreon] Major Change to Patreon Platform... Again
posted on 02.02.2026 05:21 by CDB-Man

Major change to Patreon Platform... Again


Hi everyone,

Unfortunately, Patreon has again announced the elimination of "per-creation billing" after November 2026 due to forced changed by Apple. You can read the announcement here: https://www.patreon.com/posts/148395613

Patreon announced this previous forced change by Apple here, then walked it back here.

AniDB currently uses a "per-creation billing" model. This is how we are able to control when we make paid Patreon posts, and collect your pledges. This control allowed AniDB to minimize the collection of excess funds beyond what we need to operate the site.

Patreon is again making this change because they have been forced to do so by Apple. Apple's app store no longer supports Patreon's per-creation billing model. In order for Patreon to comply and remain on the Apple App Store, they must migrate all creators to a Subscription model.

As a result of this change from Patreon, AniDB will be migrating to a subscription model in mid-2026. The November 2025 banner contest and paid post will likely be the last one under the current "per-creation billing" model.

We are currently assessing the impact of this change to AniDB, and what this means for everyone that supports us. Most likely, the following will happen:
  1. We move to a monthly subscription model as required by Patreon
  2. Everyone adjusts their Patreon pledges to a smaller amount to account for monthly billing
  3. Patreon also offers the option of annual subscription -- we are reviewing this feature
  4. If AniDB accumulates more money than we need, we will use Patreon's "pause monthly billing" feature to pause billings for 1 month at a time: https://support.patreon.com/hc/en-us/articles/214013323-How-to-pause-billing-for-your-members
  5. We keep pausing each month, as needed, until we burn off our surplus funds


Based on our current hosting costs, we need approximately $3,150 USD per year ($262 USD per month) to keep the lights on.

Last time we ran a poll, none of AniDB's supporters use the iOS app. Unfortunately, there is no opt-out despite this.

We will post more details and plans as information is available. Until then, please keep following this thread.

Please keep all discussion regarding this matter on the AniDB Forum in this thread.

This post on Patreon: https://www.patreon.com/posts/149721236/
